Our History PICTO immediate concern is the laying down of the foundations for the construction of the Philippine Information Infrastructure, the country`s component of the Global Information Infrastructure that shall digitally link the entired world early this 21st century. The Infrastructure shall provide the means to enable most Filipinos, if not all, to have access to the internet and basic telephony services. PICTO shall likewise adhere to the 21st century economic dictum that private enterprise is the key engine of economic growth
